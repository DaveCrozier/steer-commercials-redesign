FAQ

Kenya import & shipping questions.

Are UK used trucks right-hand drive, like Kenyan trucks?

Yes. The UK drives on the left, the same as Kenya, so UK-sourced trucks are right-hand drive as standard — no steering conversion is needed.

Is there an age limit on used trucks imported into Kenya?

Kenya applies a maximum age limit to imported used vehicles, commonly cited as 8 years from the year of first registration. Limits and enforcement can change, so always confirm current KRA and NTSA requirements with your clearing agent before ordering.

Which port do trucks arrive at?

Trucks exported from the UK to Kenya are shipped to the Port of Mombasa, the main gateway for onward transport to Nairobi and along the Northern Corridor to Uganda, Rwanda and beyond.

Should I ship RoRo or in a container?

Most standalone tractor units and rigids move cost-effectively by RoRo. Trailers, tippers, or vehicles carrying spare parts and tools are often better protected in a container. We advise on the best method per order.

Import rules and age limits change — this page is a starting guide, not customs advice. Confirm current requirements with KRA, NTSA or your clearing agent before purchase.
